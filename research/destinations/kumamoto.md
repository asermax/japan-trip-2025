## Food Culture

**Basashi (Horse Meat Sashimi)** - Kumamoto's signature dish introduced by Kato Kiyomasa, available at most izakayas throughout the city. [ğŸ”—](https://kumamoto-guide.jp/)

**Kumamoto Ramen** - Distinct regional style featuring roasted garlic oil, with Kokutei (founded 1957) being the most popular establishment. [ğŸ”—](https://kumamoto-guide.jp/)

**Karashi Renkon** - Lotus root stuffed with mustard-miso paste, a regional specialty reflecting samurai-era food preservation techniques. [ğŸ”—](https://kumamoto-guide.jp/)

**Akaushi Wagyu** - Local red wagyu beef available throughout the city at premium establishments. [ğŸ”—](https://kumamoto-guide.jp/)
